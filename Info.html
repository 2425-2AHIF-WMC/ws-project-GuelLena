ITZY (있지) is a 5-member South Korean girl group under JYP Entertainment,
consisting of Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Yuna. They debuted on February 11, 2019
with their first single album, IT’z Different.Text. Group Name Meaning: The word ‘있지’ translates to English as “to have”, which means that the group members have everything people expect in them. This ties in to their introduction.
